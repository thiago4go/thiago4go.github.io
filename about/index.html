About

Welcome to "Back to the Dev" 🚀

I'm Thiago, an ICT developer who loves technology, specifically cloud-native applications. 🌐 This blog name originated from my journey back to the core of software development.

I started coding in high school with assembly language. Even if life took me in other directions, those early days fostered a lifelong interest in computers and programming. As AI has become mainstream, I'm back to my roots, diving deep into programming, and I'm here to learn and share my opinions. 💻

With over a decade of tech expertise, I've explored many technologies, but Kubernetes is my current focus. My international upbringing—born in Brazil, spent years in Japan, and now in Australia—has profoundly impacted my approach to technology and problem-solving. 🌏

I deliver high-quality, scalable solutions at Designs To You in Melbourne, Victoria, using my full-stack development skills in Linux, Python, Laravel, PHP, and Tailwind. Outside of work, I co-host a Kubernetes User Group to discuss and demystify container orchestration. 👨‍💻

When I'm not working, playing Go and solving magic cubes improve my strategic thinking and problem-solving skills. These hobbies not only offer a mental escape but also enhance my technical abilities, which I apply in my day-to-day work. 🧠

"Back to the Dev" is more than a blog—it's a record of my return to programming and a place to share technical knowledge, especially about AI and Kubernetes. Join me on this voyage of learning, sharing, and exploring the endless potential of technology, whether you're a tech enthusiast, an aspiring developer, or just IT-curious. 🚢
